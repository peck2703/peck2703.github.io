About Ujamaa-Jam-A

This was my most ambitious project ever done for a game jam, and as such, is still in need of some personal touches. This theme was also the most difficult, as it was meant to represent at least one of the seven principles of Kwanzaa (mine was meant to represent Ujamaa, meaning "Cooperative Economics"). The idea being that each of the five merchants (Food, Lumber, Stone, Iron, and Transporters) are required to help each other ("the cooperative part") in order to progress to the highest level. Each successive level brings in more materials (or transports in the case of the transporter).
