About Raid on Santa's Village

This game was a submission to the Winter Game Jam for 2020, hosted by one of our professors and was open to all students and alumni. The theme for This game jam was Christmas Nightmare. The idea was to have the elves try to unionize and Krampus was called in by the big guy (Santa) to put down the strike with the use of his "surprise" present launcher, nicknamed "Santa's Baby".

Put down the strike and get those Elves back to work to make the children of the world happy again!
