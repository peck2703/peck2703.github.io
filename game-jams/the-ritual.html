About The Ritual

This game was a submission to the BitBridge Halloween Game Jam that was based out of Pittsburgh, PA. All of the participants in this project were all students and alumni of my alma mater, Southern New Hampshire University, including one of our professors. The subjects for this game jam was either ritual or harvest, so we chose to go with the ritual keyword, literally. The story behind the game is that you are a streamer who studies ancient and abandoned chapels and monasteries, but discover that this one is still being used to hold satanic rituals and your life is dependent on whether or not you can subvert the ritual by taking out the cultists.

To your dismay, the demon spawns anyways and chases you, can you make it out of the monastary alive?
