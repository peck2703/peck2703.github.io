About Me

My name is Michael Peck, and I am a video game programmer with experience across many disciplines, including gameplay mechanics, user interface, and artificial intelligence programming. I have years of experience in leading small-scale teams in an agile environment in multiple environments, including educational and professional.

Being a programmer with this wide variety of expertise allows me a myriad of opportunities to learn and grow in each area. Working on building a new gameplay mechanic is one of the most fulfilling aspects of programming for me. Seeing the already endless array of possibilities and then creating your own gives you a feeling of limitless potential is what drives me to explore these new ideas.

Working with a small indie game studio has given me the chance to experiment with all the aspects of programming, including extensive menu building, level sequencers, or cinematics, and developing an AI class that's smart enough to track the player in more ways than one. Working in a small team makes it easier to train the other team members in the Scrum/Agile methodology and plan for our sprints more efficiently.

I am currently working as a Lead Programmer and Build Master with Belfrost Studios, but if you are looking for someone with experience in game programming and would like to know more, you may contact me here or visit any of my links.
